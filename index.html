About Me

I graduated from San Diego State University in 2016 and have been trying to figure out what to do with the rest of my life. I was born in San Diego but moved out to Chicago, IL where I completed 5th - 8th grade when my Dad got stationed there for the Navy. I then moved to Hawaii my freshman year of high school after my Dad finished his rotation(?) in Chicago. I only stayed in Hawaii for a year and ended up returning to San Diego to finish high school. After high school I began school at SDSU with a mechanical engineering major, I ended up switching out of ME into CompE because tech interested me more. While at SDSU I became involved with the professional service fraternity APO. At APO I took on executive board roles, vice president of membership. Post college has been quite the struggle. I managed turn my college internship into a full time job which I stayed at for two years. In the end I learned the DoD work is just not for me and I have to put myself out there to try something new. My experience so far has primarily been in testing, and I am hoping to eventually get into software development. At what kind of company? I am really not sure.
